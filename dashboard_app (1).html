Actionable Recommendations
Business Insights · Priority Ranked
01
High Priority
Target At-Risk Segment with Proactive CS Outreach
316 customers in the At-Risk segment show 23.1% churn with high support ticket volumes. Assign dedicated customer success managers to customers with >3 tickets in the past 30 days. A churn prevention program targeting this segment could recover ~$560K in ARR (316 × $1,771 avg LTV × 23.1% churn rate).
02
High Priority
Fix the Onboarding Funnel — Close the Invite Teammate Gap
The funnel analysis (MongoDB onboarding_events) shows 68% drop-off at the "Invite Teammate" step. Team adoption drives stickiness — solo users churn faster. Add an in-app prompt with a guided teammate invitation flow immediately after first project creation. Even a 20% improvement in this step would meaningfully lift 90-day retention.
03
Medium Priority
Shift Acquisition Budget from Events to Partner Channel
Partner-acquired customers churn at only 15.1% vs event-acquired at 26.6% — an 11.5 percentage point difference. This is the single largest acquisition channel quality gap in the data (SQL: customers.signup_source). Reallocating 20% of event marketing budget to partner co-marketing programs would improve cohort LTV and reduce churn without changing the product.
